Hack the NECSTCamp Event 2018

What's about?

As part of the new edition of the PEoPLe@DEIB events, the NECSTCamp hackathon is a 48 hours event organized by the NECSTLab in order to turn data into action and help athletes, sport practitioners, and trainers to make better decision faster.

On October 27 and 28, the teams (composed by 3 students at maximum) will compete in a 48-hours hackathon at DEIB. From the hackathon to December, the 7 best teams will be selected to a second phase, where each team will be asked to work at NECSTLab with mentors working in the hackathon topics, to develop the Proof Of Concept implemented while attending the October hackathon. The winner of the second phase, will be announced on December 21st and will be awarded with the opportunity of joining the NECST Group Conference (May 17 - June 2 @ San Francisco).

To register yourself, as a single participant, or as a team, use the following form: https://tinyurl.com/hackthenecstcamp2018.
